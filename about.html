What is this graph?

The lines on the graph are a representation of the recursive sequence defined to the right.

In words: the first term is 0, and we calculate the next term by squaring the previous term, and adding the constant c.

c here is whichever coordinate your mouse is hovering over.

Z0 = 0

Zn = Zn-12 + c

For example

Let c = 1.

Then we have
Z0 = 0
Z1 = 02 + 1 = 1
Z2 = 12 + 1 = 2

Z0 = 0

Zn = Zn-12 + c

In words: the first term is 0. We calculate the next term by squaring the previous term, and adding a constant c. (Where c here is wherever your mouse is on the plane.)

These terms themselves form the vertices of the shape that is graphed when your mouse is hovering over c.
